We are LSI Engineering & Consultants Limited

Innovative Consulting Solutions for Your Business

LSI Engineering & Consultants Limited has, in a very short period of time, build a strong pan India presence.

The company is registered with Indian Bank Association (IBA) as an Agency for Specialized Monitoring (ASM) and is handling project monitoring, cash monitoring etc. for various industries on behalf of esteemed financial institutions of the country.

During its 25+ years in the industry, LSI served several mining sector clients in the capacity of a financial consultant. Further, in a bid to expand our Engineering & Consultants practise we have begun providing core business & technical services to mining & metals sector companies.

Within a short period our Mining & Metals business unit has forged partnerships with several mining technical & digital service providers to offers end to end solutioning for client problems.

We have built a team of highly talented & experienced people coming from both the industry & consulting domain. This team augmented by the expertise of our partners we bring forward an unparalleled understanding of the mining & metals industry.

Our Mining & Metals business unit offers a gamut of services ranging from project management, technical consulting, digitalization, cost optimization, blue collar skilling, mine electrification & so on.

Chairman Message

LSI Engineering, being part of larger LSI Group possesses integrated skillset of Finance, Engineering, Consulting, Business Management & Organizational Development and is in a unique position to provide integrated solutions to all the problems Concessionaires, Contractors, Corporates, Financial Institutions / Investors face in Engineering and Consulting Domains.

Senior Leadership of the Company has experience of working with Private Equity Funds, Financial Institutions, Mining & Metals & Concessionaire / Contractors, thereby enabling LSI Engineering to provide a well-rounded & balanced solutions to the problems being faced on an Asset / Project.

Mission

To be recognized as a company that successfully provides diverse services to a various segment of Customers such as Private Developers, Concessionaires, Financial Institutions and Government Authorities.

Vision

The Vision of the Company is to be one of the Leading Consulting Services with a strong Knowledge Base to positively contribute towards Development of High-Quality Sustainable practices across the globe.
